Zieler Art Supplies


More than just an Online Art Supplier

Art has always been a vital part of our offering at Lorimers and we are proud to be a stockist of Zieler Art Supplies.

Our customers love everything this UK art brand stands for – quality products that are exceptionally well presented. If you are looking for cheap art supplies Zieler may not be for you, but if you are looking for that something extra perhaps as a gift or that special occasion Zieler products are sensibly priced and are an inspiration to any budding artist.


Trusted by Artists

The main difference with Zieler compared to other art materials we stock at Lorimers is that its products, have been tested and approved by artists!

Often with a new purchase, it’s difficult knowing the quality of art paints, brushes and even paper until you’ve actually used the items by which time it’s too late to return them if you’re not happy with them! So with Zieler our customers are always reassured by the fact that artists have given their stamp of approval.

Zieler acrylic paint pens have been perhaps the most popular with our customers closely followed by the kit for lino printing.


Help with Art during Covid-19

In these difficult times with Covid-19, people have looked to art as a relaxing pass time. With all of our shops closed many of our customers were looking to start painting, drawing and sketching but had no idea of which products they would need.

Zieler has been there to support our customers on everything from how to get started with watercolour painting to which calligraphy set they should consider. If this was not enough, they also put on free art classes for people to follow and make the most of their purchase.

Hopefully, it will not be too much longer before our customers can return to our shops and get their hands on Zieler supplies as well as everything else we offer.

Trading for many years as JH Lorimer. Lorimers is a family-owned business operating from 12 branches across Surrey and Kent.

Over the years we've grown, developed and extended our offering to provide the very best in stationery, artist materials, greeting cards and toys.

With our numerous special offers, our range and vast product knowledge and our commitment to giving you the very best customer service, you can rely on Lorimers.
